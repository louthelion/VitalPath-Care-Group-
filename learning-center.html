Learning Center

Learn before you request, apply, or coordinate.

VitalPath education helps families, facilities, care professionals, and drivers understand what information to prepare before moving forward.

Learning topics

Facility staffing readiness

What facilities should prepare before requesting coverage: worker type, shift details, duties, supervision, credential needs, contract requirements, and urgency.

Care professional readiness

How CNA, HHA, RN, LPN, caregivers, companions, and sitters can prepare license/certificate details, CPR/BLS, references, resume, availability, and service area.

Transportation safety

What families and drivers should prepare for non-emergency transportation: mobility needs, schedule, rider instructions, insurance, registration, and safe communication.

Family care planning

How families can organize support notes, emergency contacts, appointment needs, wellness check-in questions, and care support priorities.

Credential verification

Why verification, background screening, insurance, contracts, and documentation matter before a case or staffing request moves forward.

Phase 1 vs active services

VitalPath can build forms, collect interest, and educate now, while active staffing or transportation work may require licensing, registration, contracts, and compliance.
