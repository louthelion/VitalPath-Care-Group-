Privacy Policy

This page explains how VitalPath Care Group may collect and use information submitted through this website.

Information collected

VitalPath may collect contact information, facility request information, care professional application information, driver application information, transportation request information, family care request information, uploaded documents, and message details submitted through website forms.

Sensitive information

Some forms may request health-related, care-related, credential-related, insurance, driver, or identity information. Submit only information you are authorized to share.

How information may be used

  • To review requests and applications.
  • To contact the person who submitted the form.
  • To evaluate credential, contract, staffing, driver, family support, or transportation coordination needs.
  • To improve internal organization and service planning.

No emergency use

This website is not for emergencies. For medical, safety, or transportation emergencies, call 911 or the proper emergency service.

Third parties

VitalPath may use website hosting, form handling, email, storage, compliance, or professional service providers. Information may be shared only as needed for review, contact, compliance, contracts, or permitted service coordination.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent through the contact page.
