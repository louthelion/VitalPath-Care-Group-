How It Works

Simple intake. Careful review. Safer coordination.

01

Submit a request or application

Facilities, families, workers, and drivers use the proper form so VitalPath can understand the need.

02

VitalPath reviews information

Requests and applications may be reviewed for location, role, credential, insurance, background screening, documents, and availability.

03

Contracts and verification come first

Before any active work, the right verification, agreements, compliance steps, insurance, registration, or licensing pathway must be in place.

VitalPath Care Group is a care support, staffing-request, caregiver application, and transportation coordination platform. Services may depend on credential verification, background screening, insurance, written contracts, licensing, registration, and availability. VitalPath does not provide emergency medical transportation, ambulance service, or medical treatment.
