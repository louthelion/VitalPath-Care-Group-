About VitalPath

Care coordination built around families, facilities, and professionals.

VitalPath Care Group is being developed as a care support, staffing-request, caregiver application, and transportation coordination platform.

Our mission

Our mission is to make care support more organized, responsive, and trustworthy by connecting the right request with the right review pathway. Facilities need dependable staffing options. Families need guidance. Professionals need opportunities. Drivers need structured transportation cases. VitalPath brings those pathways together.

Vision

To become a trusted care coordination network where facilities, families, care professionals, and drivers can connect through verified, documented, and compassionate systems.

Objective

To build a platform that receives requests, applications, credentials, documents, availability, and service needs before any assignment, contract, or case is considered.

Who we serve

Clinics, hospitals, nursing homes, assisted living facilities, agencies, families, seniors, disabled adults, disabled children, caregivers, nurses, aides, companions, and drivers.

How we protect the company

VitalPath is written in Phase 1 language until the proper registration, licensing, contracts, verification, insurance, and compliance steps are complete.

VitalPath does not provide emergency medical transportation, ambulance service, medical treatment, nursing care, or staffing placement unless the proper licensing, registration, contracts, and compliance structure are in place.
